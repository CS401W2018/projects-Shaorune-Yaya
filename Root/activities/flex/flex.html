  2. Why do you need to have an accessible website? What does that mean?

    3. An accessible website means that the content and features of the website are available to all users, regardless of whether they have any kind of disabilities. For some other languages, they might read from right to left, for flexbox, we can use row-reverse or column-reverse to make the nav readable from right to left
