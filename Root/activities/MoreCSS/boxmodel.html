  2. What is the margin, border, and padding?

    • Margin: The space outside an element, used to control the distance between the element and other elements.
    • Border: The edge of an element that can be set in width, style, and color, surrounding the content and inner margins of the element.
    • Padding: The space inside an element, located between the content and the border, controls the distance between the content and the border.
