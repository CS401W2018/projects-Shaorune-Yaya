Shanghai Aquarium

The Shanghai Aquarium is located in Lujiazui, Pudong and is one of the largest modern aquariums in China.
There are multiple exhibition areas in the museum, showcasing marine life from around the world, and the world's longest underwater sightseeing tunnel, making visitors feel as if they are in a world of the ocean.
The Shanghai Aquarium not only showcases marine life, but also focuses on marine environmental protection and education, which is deeply loved by tourists.
