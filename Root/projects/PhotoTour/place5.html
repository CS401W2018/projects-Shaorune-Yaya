Lujiazui Finance&Trade Zone

Lujiazui Financial City is located in Pudong New Area, Shanghai.
It is China's first national level development zone named after "finance and trade", and also the core functional area of Shanghai International Financial Center.
This place gathers numerous multinational banks' China and East Asia headquarters, as well as important financial institutions such as the Shanghai Stock Exchange.
