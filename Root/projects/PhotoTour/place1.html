Yuyuan Garden

Founded in the Ming Dynasty, the Yu Garden in Shanghai has a history of more than 400 years.
It is a typical classical garden in the south of the Yangtze River.
The park integrates elements such as pavilions, terraces, ponds, rockeries, and winding corridors, with exquisite and unique designs.
As a representative of Chinese traditional garden art, Yu Garden is a must visit place to understand Shanghai's history and culture.
