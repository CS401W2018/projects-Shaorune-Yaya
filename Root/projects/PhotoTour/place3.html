Shanghai DisneyLand

Shanghai Disneyland is the first Disneyland theme park in Chinese Mainland, located in Pudong New Area of Shanghai.
The park has multiple unique themed areas, including the Fantasy Fairy Tale Castle, Treasure Cove, and Tomorrow's World, suitable for visitors of all ages.
Not only does it offer classic Disney entertainment projects, but it also integrates rich Chinese cultural elements, bringing a unique gaming experience.
