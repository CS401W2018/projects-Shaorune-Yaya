Oriental Pearl TV Tower

Located in Lujiazui, Pudong, Shanghai, the Oriental Pearl TV Tower Radio and Television Tower is one of the landmark buildings in Shanghai.
The tower is 468 meters high and has multiple observation levels, providing a panoramic view overlooking the entire city.
As a multi-functional building integrating radio and television transmission, sightseeing, catering and entertainment, the Oriental Pearl TV Tower Tower has attracted a large number of tourists.
