LinaBell is a little fox full of wisdom, curiosity, and an adventurous spirit. She is a member of the Disney Duffy family. She made her first show on September 29, 2021, and was officially released at Shanghai Disneyland. This cute little fox quickly won the love of many fans in China and other parts of Asia.

LinaBell's fame is inseparable from her unique personality and cute image. She has a charming appearance, and her intelligent, often using her magnifying glass to explore and solve problems, showing her witty side. Her role is defined as an explorer who loves nature and explores the unknown.
