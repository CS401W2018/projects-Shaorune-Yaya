Starting tips for new cat owners

  • Make your cat curious by using treats
  • Use your finger tell your cat where is the treats
  • Gradually approach your cat and reward
  • Sit on the floor and use finger to guide it
  • Practice and build trust and relationship



The article "How to train a cat" by Zazie Todd emphasizes that the key to scientifically training cats is to understand their learning methods and use positive reinforcement. Cats understand the relationship between behavior and outcomes through associative learning and operant conditioning. Therefore, when cats exhibit expected behavior, immediately reinforcing this behavior with rewards (such as food or praise) is the most effective method. Training should be kept brief and frequent, and the cat's favorite reward should be chosen to enhance the training effect. In addition, avoid using punishment as it may lead to fear or stress, which can actually hinder learning progress. Teaching practical skills, such as entering a cat cage, can also alleviate daily challenges and improve the adaptability and quality of life of cats.
